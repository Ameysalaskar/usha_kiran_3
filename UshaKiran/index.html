Overview

UshaKiran Residency offers a perfect blend of modern living and everyday convenience in the heart of Badlapur East.


Just 2 km from the railway station, our 12-storied twin towers—spread across a 10-acre township—feature OC Received, Ready to Move-in 2 BHK homes with top-tier amenities and double lift access.


With 85% of families already moved in, the project also includes a 21,000 sq. ft. commercial complex, ensuring daily needs are close at hand. Designed for comfort, connectivity and quality family time, UshaKiran Residency is your gateway to elevated living.
